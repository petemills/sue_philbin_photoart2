ME AND MY PHOTOS
ME

I'm a freelance photographer living in glorious New Zealand. Down here we have an abundance of stunning vistas and gorgeous backdrops, making photography a wonderfully rewarding acivity.

I live in the Waikato with my family anfd like nothing more than venturing off into the unknown, camera in hand and dogs by my side!

MY PHOTOS

I take photographs with a few core idioms:

Keep it clean

Pick a subject that needs to be seen and remembered

Find the angle and lighting that brings what I'm seeing to life

MY SERVICES
Weddings + Events
I am available for weddings and any other occasions you may be planning! When it comes to photographing people and events, I look to capture more than just smiles and faces. I endevour to find the heart and soul in where ever and what ever I'm shooting and this results in unorthodox, spur of the moment photography that captures memorable, yet natural moments on camera.
Prints + Calenders
Any of the of high quality photographs I have accrued over the years can be purchased as lavish prints. For print runs of more than 60, I can also design and produce customised calenders. Should you desire a calender designed, I offer a free consultation to develop and realise any ideas you may have with regards to subject matter or photo selection. See the gallery below for some examples of my work.
Little Books
My main focus recently has been shooting for and designing my own brand of photography books. The idea behind these 'Little Books' is simple: I want to capture the essence of a place, city, town or region using photos rather than words, then wrap this essence up in a quaint little book. The books make ideal gifts and sit wonderfully on bookshelves, amoungst all those other 'Big Books'! Currently I have three little books on store shelves; documenting with pictures: Hamilton, Cambridge and Raglan.

Don't hesitate to contact me should you have a region, town or city you want immortalised in a little book! As with all my work, I offer a free consultation to get the ball rolling.
